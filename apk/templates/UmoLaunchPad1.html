Umo space is a top down space adventure RPG shooter by Andrew Kane.

Features:

  • Procedurally generated solar systems, enemies, missions, and cargo prices make for a unique experience every time you play.
  • Dockable stations with ship upgrades to buy, cargo to trade, and missions to take.
  • 10 unique and upgradeable weapons
  • Ship equipment and weapon upgrades
  • Wormholes allowing the player to travel through the 3rd dimension
  • Artisanal game and physics engine governs both players and celestial bodies
  • Detailed HUD including zoomable map, compass, radar, targeting, ship statistics, cargo manifest, and much more.
  • Wireless
  • Non-GMO
  • Lead-Free

    • Ambitions:
  • Multiplayer
  • Music
  • Even more of everything it already has

    • Controls:
  • Use mouse to steer ship (it points at your cursor), left click to fire weapons, right click to fire thrusters. Click, don't hold.
  • Remember to slow down before you reach (or overshoot) your destination
  • J will toggle journal, showing all past radio messages. Spacebar will skip to end of current message.
  • Click (shoot) the "controls" planet in the menu system for a complete list of controls.
