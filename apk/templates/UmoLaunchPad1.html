Umo space is a top down space adventure RPG shooter by Andrew Kane.

Features:

  • 1 complete solar system
  • Procedurally generated enemies
  • 10 unique weapons
  • Dockable Stations
  • Ship equipment and weapon upgrades
  • Interactive HUD
  • Wireless
  • Non-GMO
  • Lead-Free

    • Ambitions:
  • Procedurally generated systems
  • Friendly NPCs
  • Trading
  • Procedural weapon upgrades

    • Controls:
  • Mouse steers ship
  • Tap spacebar for thrust
  • Control key fires the current weapon
  • S key to toggle shop menu
  • N key to cycle nav targets
